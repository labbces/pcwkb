Welcome to Plant Cell Wall KnowledgeBase!

Our database facilitates the connection between molecular biology experiments and changes in the composition of plant cell wall and biomass.
It mainly comprises grass species (including the main grass C3 and C4 models), but we also include dicot models (such as Arabidopsis thaliana).
We expect that this resource accelerates research aiming to study elements involved in biomass recalcitrance, energy production, and any other cell wall-related research you wish.
