Welcome to Plant Cell Wall KnowledgeBase!

Our database facilitates the conection between molecular biology experiments and changes in composition of plant cell wall and biomass.
It mainly comprises grass species (including the main grass C3 and C4 models), but we also include dicot models (such as Arabidopsis thaliana).
We expect that this resource acelerate research aiming to study elements involved in biomass recalcitrance and finally use this information in molecular breeding programs.
